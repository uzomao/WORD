We introduce WIDOWS, ORPHANS & DESTITUTES (WORD) EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION, a registered (RC: 87756) non-governmental organization that aims to empower Widows, Orphans and the poor through education, vocation and start-up capitals.The Board of Trustees of WORD is made up of credible Nigerians with diverse experience in Counseling, Business Administration, Legal Services, Education, Agriculture, Banking and Government.

A B O U T

Organizational History

WORD has been involved in reaching out to her targeted beneficiaries since December 2003 with counseling and material needs and welfare. Since then, we have reached out to 101 widows through our various activities.

Our Vision

Adding value to the lives of widows and orphans To empower identifiable widows, orphans and destitute with life skill acquisition to enable them make a living (in order to add value to their lives).

Our Mission

We wish to better the lives of our beneficiaries. Reaching out to widows, orphans and destitutes in order to better their lives.

Our Approach

Our approach in fulfilling our Vision is through:

  • Vocational training/skill acquisition
  • Financial literacy education
  • Health care support
  • Legal aid/representation
  • Agricultural aid
  • Financial empowerment.

Current Activities

  • Training on Financial Literacy
  • Skill acquisition training
  • Dispense of Soft Loan through a Micro Financial Bank
