Keeping Hope Alive

We introduce WIDOWS, ORPHANS & DESTITUTES (WORD) EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION, a registered (RC: 87756) non-governmental organization that aims to empower Widows, Orphans and the poor through education, vocation and start-up capital.The Board of Trustees of WORD is made up of credible Nigerians with diverse experience in Counseling, Business Administration, Legal Services, Education, Agriculture, Banking and Government.

ABOUT

Organizational History

WORD has been involved in reaching out to her targeted beneficiaries since December 2003 with counseling and material needs and welfare. Since then, we have reached out to 101 widows through our various activities.

Our Vision

Adding value to the lives of identifiable widows, orphans and destitutes through various life skill acquisition and financial empowerment.

Our Mission

We simply wish to change the lives of our beneficiaries for the better.

Our Approach

  • Vocational training/skill acquisition
  • Financial literacy education
  • Health care support
  • Legal aid/representation
  • Agricultural aid
  • Financial empowerment
